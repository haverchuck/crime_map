About

The developers of this application love Seattle and wanted to find ways to keep their fellow Seattleites informed. Crime Map App shows exactly when and where a crime has occurred in Seattle.

How do we do this? Simple. The data comes directly from the Seattle Police Department's database. Our app simply offers a way to visually take note of the crimes that occur on a daily basis, not so different from how you would search for restaurants on Google Maps. We also make use of Twitter's API to show you tweets from around the time and area the crime occurred.

For instructions on how to use Crime Map App, please click on the "How To" section.
